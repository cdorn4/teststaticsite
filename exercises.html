Complete the following challenges to prepare for your trip to Greece!

🗺️ Mission: The Parthenon Lunch Run

Scenario: You are on a class trip to Athens, and you've just finished touring the breathtaking Parthenon! Your group has exactly 30 minutes before you need to meet up for the next activity. You are starving and need to grab a quick, delicious local bite for lunch, but you don't want to settle for just any tourist trap.

📍 Open the Parthenon in Google Maps

The Constraints (You must meet ALL of these):

  • Location: It must be less than a 10-minute walk from the Parthenon.
  • Quality: The restaurant must have a Google Maps rating of over 4.5 stars.
  • Popularity: It must have over 500 reviews.
  • Menu: They must serve local, quick Greek food (gyros, souvlaki, pita, etc.).

Instructions:

  1. Open Google Maps on your device.
  2. Search for the Parthenon in Athens, Greece.
  3. Use the "Search nearby" feature or type in keywords like "gyro" or "souvlaki".
  4. Filter or check the results to ensure the rating is > 4.5 and reviews are > 500.
  5. Use the "Directions" feature (set to 🚶‍♂️ Walking) from the Parthenon to the restaurant to verify it is under a 10-minute walk.

Deliverable: Once you find a spot that matches all the criteria, write down: The name of the restaurant, star rating, number of reviews, exact walking time from the Parthenon, and the name of one food item you would order.

Bonus Points: Find the place with the highest number of reviews that still fits the criteria!
