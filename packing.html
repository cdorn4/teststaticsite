Packing Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes: You will be walking a lot in places like Athens and ancient ruins!
  • Modest clothing: Required for visiting temples and monasteries (shoulders and knees must be covered).
  • Sun protection: Sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat.
  • Power adapter: Type C or F plug adapters are needed for Greece.
  • Water bottle: A reusable water bottle to stay hydrated.
