High School Trip to Greece! 🇬🇷

Your ultimate travel guide and advice for our upcoming adventure.

Packing Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes: You will be walking a lot in places like Athens and ancient ruins!
  • Modest clothing: Required for visiting temples and monasteries (shoulders and knees must be covered).
  • Sun protection: Sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat.
  • Power adapter: Type C or F plug adapters are needed for Greece.
  • Water bottle: A reusable water bottle to stay hydrated.

Cultural Tips & Expectations

  • Language: Learn a few basic Greek words like "Yassas" (Hello/Goodbye) and "Efharisto" (Thank you).
  • Plumbing: In many parts of Greece, you should not flush toilet paper down the toilet; use the provided bins instead.
  • Respect: Always be respectful of historical monuments. Do not touch ancient stones or climb on ruins.
  • Curfew/Buddy System: Always stick with your assigned buddy and adhere to the evening curfews set by chaperones.

🗺️ Mission: The Pantheon Sandwich Run

Scenario: You are on a class trip to Rome, and you've just finished touring the breathtaking Pantheon! Your group has exactly 30 minutes before you need to meet up for the next activity. You are starving and need to grab a quick, delicious sandwich for lunch, but you don't want to settle for just any tourist trap.

The Constraints (You must meet ALL of these):

  • Location: It must be less than a 10-minute walk from the Pantheon.
  • Quality: The restaurant must have a Google Maps rating of over 4.5 stars.
  • Popularity: It must have over 500 reviews.
  • Menu: They must serve sandwiches (panini, schiacciata, tramezzini, etc.).

Instructions:

  1. Open Google Maps on your device.
  2. Search for the Pantheon in Rome, Italy.
  3. Use the "Search nearby" feature or type in keywords like "sandwich" or "panini".
  4. Filter or check the results to ensure the rating is > 4.5 and reviews are > 500.
  5. Use the "Directions" feature (set to 🚶‍♂️ Walking) from the Pantheon to the restaurant to verify it is under a 10-minute walk.

Deliverable: Once you find a spot that matches all the criteria, write down: The name of the restaurant, star rating, number of reviews, exact walking time from the Pantheon, and the name of one sandwich you would order.

Bonus Points: Find the place with the highest number of reviews that still fits the criteria!
